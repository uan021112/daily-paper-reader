## 摘要
基于单目相机的众包协作建图有望在无需专用传感器的情况下实现可扩展的 3D 重建，但仍受限于两种特定于尺度的失效模式：重复环境中由于误报回环检测导致的突发性尺度崩溃，以及长轨迹上的渐进式尺度漂移和单机器人尺度歧义，这些问题阻碍了直接的多会话融合。我们提出了 MR.ScaleMaster，这是一个针对众包单目视频的协作建图系统，旨在解决上述两种失效模式。MR.ScaleMaster 引入了三个关键机制：首先，尺度崩溃警报（Scale Collapse Alarm）在伪回环破坏位姿图之前将其剔除；其次，Sim(3) 锚节点公式将经典的 SE(3) 框架推广，以显式估计每个会话的尺度，从而解决单机器人尺度歧义并强制执行全局尺度一致性；第三，模块化、开源且即插即用的接口使任何单目重建模型都能在无需修改后端的情况下进行集成。在包含多达 15 个智能体的 KITTI 序列上，Sim(3) 公式相比 SE(3) 基准实现了 7.2 倍的绝对轨迹误差（ATE）降低，且警报在保留所有有效约束的同时剔除了一切误报回环。我们进一步展示了异构多机器人稠密建图，在单一统一地图中融合了 MASt3R-SLAM、pi3 和 VGGT-SLAM 2.0。

## Abstract
Crowd-sourced cooperative mapping from monocular cameras promises scalable 3D reconstruction without specialized sensors, yet remains hindered by two scale-specific failure modes: abrupt scale collapse from false-positive loop closures in repetitive environments, and gradual scale drift over long trajectories and per-robot scale ambiguity that prevent direct multi-session fusion. We present \textbf{MR.ScaleMaster}, a cooperative mapping system for crowd-sourced monocular videos that addresses both failure modes. \textbf{MR.ScaleMaster} introduces three key mechanisms. First, a Scale Collapse Alarm rejects spurious loop closures before they corrupt the pose graph. Second, a Sim(3) anchor node formulation generalizes the classical SE(3) framework to explicitly estimate per-session scale, resolving per-robot scale ambiguity and enforcing global scale consistency. Third, a modular, open-source, plug-and-play interface enables any monocular reconstruction model to integrate without backend modification. On KITTI sequences with up to 15 agents, the Sim(3) formulation achieves a 7.2x ATE reduction over the SE(3) baseline, and the alarm rejects all false-positive loops while preserving every valid constraint. We further demonstrate heterogeneous multi-robot dense mapping fusing MASt3R-SLAM, pi3, and VGGT-SLAM 2.0 within a single unified map.

    1.  **突发性尺度崩溃/爆炸**：在走廊等重复性环境中，误报的回环检测（False-positive loops）会导致优化器为了满足错误约束而剧烈扭曲尺度，使地图缩减至零或趋于无穷。

### 2. 提出的方法论
MR.ScaleMaster 引入了三个核心机制来构建尺度一致的全局地图：
